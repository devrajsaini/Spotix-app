Why Spottix?

Everything you need to make your
city cleaner

Spottix combines AI-powered civic tech with community action to solve real urban problems faster than ever.

Instant Action

Reports reach municipal authorities in seconds. No emails, no waiting — just direct action on civic issues.

Full Transparency

Track every report from submission to resolution. Authorities are accountable and citizens are informed.

Community Network

Over 2,000 active citizens working together. Strength in numbers drives faster resolutions and cleaner wards.

Real-Time Data

Live dashboards show issue hotspots, resolution rates, and ward performance updated every minute.

Privacy First

Anonymous reporting available. Your identity is protected while your report still drives real change.

Authority Integration

BBMP ward officers, BWSSB, BESCOM — all connected. Reports go to exactly the right department.
